# User Personas

This document outlines the core user personas for ShopSense AI to ensure design and engineering decisions align with user needs.

## 1. The Student (Riya)
- **Goals:** Find the absolute best value for money; discover hidden student discounts.
- **Pain Points:** Limited budget, overwhelmed by options, afraid of buying outdated tech.
- **Shopping Habits:** Spends hours researching, watching YouTube reviews, and waiting for sales.
- **Technical Knowledge:** High.
- **Buying Behaviour:** Highly price-sensitive, impulsive only during massive sales (Big Billion Days).
- **Preferred Devices:** Mobile (90%), Budget Laptop.
- **Budget:** Low.
- **Motivations:** Maximizing utility per rupee spent.
- **Frustrations:** Fake discounts, expired coupons.

## 2. The Software Engineer (Rahul)
- **Goals:** Find the best specifications and latest tech without wasting time.
- **Pain Points:** Hates cluttered UI, slow websites, and inaccurate spec sheets.
- **Shopping Habits:** Knows exactly what he wants, filters aggressively by specs.
- **Technical Knowledge:** Expert.
- **Buying Behaviour:** Analytical, fast decision-maker once data is verified.
- **Preferred Devices:** Desktop / Dual Monitors.
- **Budget:** High.
- **Motivations:** Performance, efficiency, clean data.
- **Frustrations:** Lack of dark mode, intrusive ads, inaccurate data.

## 3. The Working Professional (Priya)
- **Goals:** Buy reliable products quickly; replace broken items instantly.
- **Pain Points:** Zero time for deep research; decision fatigue.
- **Shopping Habits:** Reads top 3 reviews and buys; relies heavily on AI summaries.
- **Technical Knowledge:** Medium.
- **Buying Behaviour:** Convenience-driven, willing to pay more for faster delivery.
- **Preferred Devices:** Mobile (Commute), Laptop (Work).
- **Budget:** Medium-High.
- **Motivations:** Saving time, trusting the platform.
- **Frustrations:** Complex checkouts, too many choices without a clear "Best" recommendation.

## 4. The Parent (Amit)
- **Goals:** Buy safe, durable products for the family and home.
- **Pain Points:** Unsure about technical jargon; relies on trust and brand reputation.
- **Shopping Habits:** Asks friends, reads user comments and negative reviews first.
- **Technical Knowledge:** Low-Medium.
- **Buying Behaviour:** Cautious, looks for warranties and return policies.
- **Preferred Devices:** Mobile.
- **Budget:** Medium.
- **Motivations:** Family well-being, durability.
- **Frustrations:** Hidden costs, poor customer service from merchants.

## 5. The Gamer (Kabir)
- **Goals:** Build the ultimate gaming rig; find the lowest price on GPUs and peripherals.
- **Pain Points:** Scalpers, out-of-stock items, misleading performance metrics.
- **Shopping Habits:** Tracks prices daily, uses multiple Discord bots for alerts.
- **Technical Knowledge:** Expert (Hardware).
- **Buying Behaviour:** Obsessive comparison, buys immediately when target price hits.
- **Preferred Devices:** Custom PC, Mobile.
- **Budget:** High.
- **Motivations:** Maximum FPS, aesthetics (RGB), latest generation hardware.
- **Frustrations:** Unreliable stock alerts, delayed price updates.

## 6. The Fashion Buyer (Sneha)
- **Goals:** Stay on trend; visually compare outfits across Myntra, Ajio, etc.
- **Pain Points:** Sizing inconsistencies across brands; hard to imagine the fit.
- **Shopping Habits:** Visual-first, heavily influenced by Instagram/Pinterest.
- **Technical Knowledge:** Medium.
- **Buying Behaviour:** Frequent small purchases, high return rate.
- **Preferred Devices:** Mobile.
- **Budget:** Medium.
- **Motivations:** Aesthetics, social validation, discovering new brands.
- **Frustrations:** Poor quality images, lack of zoom, inaccurate color representation.

## 7. The Mobile Buyer (Vikram)
- **Goals:** Upgrade to the best phone for his budget (Camera/Battery focus).
- **Pain Points:** Too many variants (RAM/Storage) confusing the price.
- **Shopping Habits:** Compares AnTuTu scores, watches camera comparisons.
- **Technical Knowledge:** High.
- **Buying Behaviour:** Waits for launch offers, credit card discounts, and exchange bonuses.
- **Preferred Devices:** Mobile.
- **Budget:** Medium-High.
- **Motivations:** Having the latest tech, social status.
- **Frustrations:** "Out of stock" during flash sales, missing bank offers.

## 8. The Laptop Buyer (Ananya)
- **Goals:** Find a reliable machine for college/work that lasts 4+ years.
- **Pain Points:** Confused by processor generations (Intel i5 12th vs 13th gen).
- **Shopping Habits:** Reads extensive buying guides, uses AI to simplify choices.
- **Technical Knowledge:** Low.
- **Buying Behaviour:** One-time high-value purchase; extreme caution.
- **Preferred Devices:** Tablet/Mobile.
- **Budget:** Medium.
- **Motivations:** Productivity, longevity, portability.
- **Frustrations:** Tech jargon, heavy laptops, poor battery life.

## 9. The Budget Shopper (Ravi)
- **Goals:** Never pay full price.
- **Pain Points:** Missing out on flash sales or limited-time coupons.
- **Shopping Habits:** Uses incognito mode, hunting for cashback, waits months for price drops.
- **Technical Knowledge:** Medium.
- **Buying Behaviour:** Highly elastic demand based on price.
- **Preferred Devices:** Mobile.
- **Budget:** Very Low.
- **Motivations:** The thrill of the deal.
- **Frustrations:** Delivery charges that negate the discount.

## 10. The Premium Shopper (Karan)
- **Goals:** Buy the absolute best (Apple, Sony, Dyson) with minimal friction.
- **Pain Points:** Counterfeit products, poor post-purchase support.
- **Shopping Habits:** Brand loyal, searches directly for the flagship model.
- **Technical Knowledge:** Medium-High.
- **Buying Behaviour:** Instant purchase if the platform is trusted.
- **Preferred Devices:** iPhone, Mac.
- **Budget:** Unlimited.
- **Motivations:** Status, flawless experience, premium build quality.
- **Frustrations:** Cluttered UI, slow loading times, untrustworthy merchants.
