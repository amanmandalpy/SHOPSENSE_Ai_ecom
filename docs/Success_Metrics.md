# Success Metrics & KPIs

This document outlines the Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) for ShopSense AI to measure business growth, product health, and user engagement.

## 1. Acquisition Metrics
- **Daily Active Users (DAU):** The number of unique users interacting with the platform daily.
- **Monthly Active Users (MAU):** The number of unique users over a 30-day period.
- **Organic Traffic Growth:** Month-over-month increase in visitors originating from search engines (driven by SEO and Blogs).
- **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC):** Total marketing/ad spend divided by new users acquired.

## 2. Activation Metrics
- **Search Success Rate:** Percentage of search queries that result in a user clicking on a product (target: > 85%).
- **Account Creation Rate:** Percentage of guest users who sign up to save alerts/wishlists.
- **Time to First Search:** How quickly a user initiates a search after landing on the site (target: < 5 seconds).

## 3. Engagement & Retention Metrics
- **Average Session Duration:** Average time spent per visit (higher means they are using the AI and comparing products).
- **Bounce Rate:** Percentage of users who leave after viewing only one page (target: < 40%).
- **Wishlist/Alert Usage Rate:** Percentage of active users utilizing the "Add to Compare" or "Set Price Alert" features.
- **AI Chat Usage:** Percentage of sessions where the user interacts with the AI Assistant.
- **Returning User Rate:** Percentage of MAU that visited the site in the previous month.

## 4. Monetization & Revenue Metrics (The Most Critical)
- **Affiliate Click-Through Rate (CTR):** The percentage of product page views that result in a click on the "Go to Store" button.
- **Conversion Rate (from Affiliate Dashboards):** Percentage of users who actually complete a purchase after clicking our link.
- **Revenue Per User (RPU):** Total affiliate revenue divided by total active users.
- **Coupon Usage Rate:** Number of clicks on "Copy Coupon" leading to an affiliate out-click.
- **Price Alert Conversion:** The percentage of triggered price alerts (emails/push) that result in a click-through to the merchant.

## 5. Technical & Performance Metrics
- **Core Web Vitals:** 
  - LCP (Largest Contentful Paint) < 2.5s
  - FID (First Input Delay) < 100ms
  - CLS (Cumulative Layout Shift) < 0.1
- **API Response Time:** Target < 200ms for 95th percentile.
- **Error Rate (5xx/4xx):** Target < 0.1% of all requests.

## 6. Content Metrics
- **Blog Traffic:** Monthly views on AI-generated and editorial buying guides.
- **Content CTR:** Percentage of blog readers who click through to a product comparison page.
